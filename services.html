Our Services

What do we do?

Here at Planet Entertainment we offer multiple services that can differ depending on what category you are interested in. For books/ comics, our “Rookie Astronaut Pathways” can help reader get into longer series, and we can send you to any website to make your purchase. For plays, we can give schedules and ticket prices to any playwright or musical happening in theaters around your area and the U.S. at the time you logged in. For Movies and Television, we give out informational reviews that lets you decide on if a movie/ show is worth your time, and our interactive articles about movies/ shows of the past will be sure to spark many conversations. For Video Games, like previous category, we have concise informational reviews of newly released games, as well as tutorial videos, tips and tricks videos, and videos that will tell you all you need to know about a video game. For Music & More, we have the latest news about the music industry and you can shop for album C.D.’s or other musical memorabilia. Our last service is the “Record Station.” Here, you can go through the history of every previous category to learn something new.
